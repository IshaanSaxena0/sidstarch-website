Welcome to Siddharth Starch Pvt. Ltd.

Based in Maharashtra, Siddharth Starch Pvt. Ltd. is a renowned manufacturer and supplier of food-grade and industrial starch. Our quality-centric manufacturing processes and customer-first approach have earned us long-term trust across sectors like food processing, textiles, corrugation, adhesives, and pharmaceuticals.

Our Journey

Founded with a vision to deliver precision-quality starch solutions, Siddharth Starch has scaled steadily with innovation and infrastructure. Our modern production unit, equipped with advanced machinery and lab testing, allows us to meet exacting standards — batch after batch.
